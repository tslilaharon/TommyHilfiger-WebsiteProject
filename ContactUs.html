When will I receive my order?
We ship Monday through Friday excluding U.S. federal holidays. Please allow up to two business days processing time in addition to standard delivery time based on your selected shipping method.
Please note, during high volume times such as holidays and special sales, there may be a slight delay in order processing. Your business is important to us, and we’ll make every effort to process your order as soon as possible.
+
What's your return policy?
For orders placed within the United States, we accept returns within 90 days (effective 4/15/2020) after you receive shipping confirmation. For orders shipped to Canada, we accept returns within 90 days after shipping confirmation. In order to accept your return, items must be unworn with tags attached. Footwear must be unworn and include its original shoebox without post labels.
Please note, items marked FINAL SALE are not eligible for returns, refunds, exchanges or credits.

What is the difference between the different Tommy Hilfiger collection lines?
-TOMMY HILFIGER-
Tommy Hilfiger is our signature sportswear line where you'll find all the classic American staples that put us on the map - our polos, chinos, oxfords, blazers and outerwear that exemplify classic American cool.
-HILFIGER COLLECTION-
This is our premiere line where classics are boldly re-imagined while remaining wearable and rooted in our preppy heritage. Each piece is made in Italy and produced in very limited quantities, while many of these styles are featured in our bi-annual runway shows.
-TOMMY JEANS-
Formerly known as Hilfiger Denim, Tommy Jeans targets our younger, edgier demographic. The line plays off the re-issue of our authentic original designs released in the 80s and 90s that are now considered 'vintage.'
-TOMMY HILFIGER TAILORED-
Our Tailored line re-imagines traditional suiting with innovative fabrics, exacting cuts and stylish details. These pieces have bolder prints, athleisure hybrids and mashups where modern sophistication meets the functional needs of today's man
-TOMMYNOW-
We're breaking the old-school rules of fashion and making it all accessible by giving instant access to our runway collections. These are our greatest hits, remixed and restyled for the digital age and shoppable the moment the lights go up.
